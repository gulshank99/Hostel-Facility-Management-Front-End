Asansol Engineering College has three Boys’ and two Girls’ Hostel inside College Campus. It’s a ragging free Campus.



Vivekanand Hostel

It’s rooms are well ventilated and spacious two seated cleaned room. Only First Year boys’ Hostel Boarders are staying in this hostel. It is a 4 storied building. Both lodging and fooding [Breakfast, Lunch, Tiffin and dinner] are available.




J.C. Bose Hostel

It’s rooms are well ventilated and spacious two seated cleaned room. 2nd Year & 3rd Year boys’ Hostel Boarders are staying in this hostel. Both lodging and fooding [Breakfast, Lunch, Tiffin and dinner] are available.




A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Hostel

It’s rooms are well ventilated and spacious two seated cleaned room. Only 4th Year boys’ Hostel Boarders are staying in this hostel. Both lodging and fooding [Breakfast, Lunch, Tiffin and dinner] are available.




Sarojini Girls’ Hostel (New Building)

It’s rooms are well ventilated and spacious two seated cleaned room. Only 1st Year girls’ Hostel Boarders are staying in this hostel. Both lodging and fooding [Breakfast, Lunch, Tiffin and dinner] are available.




Sarojini Girls’ Hostel (Old Building)

It’s rooms are well ventilated and spacious two seated cleaned room. The 2nd Year, 3rd Year & Final Year girls’ Hostel Boarders are staying in this hostel. Both lodging and fooding [Breakfast, Lunch, Tiffin and dinner] are available.




Play Ground

The well-maintained sports facilities in the college hostel playground provide students with opportunities to play cricket, football, basketball, and other popular sports.






Facilities

Facilities for all Hostels :

  1. Well ventilated spacious double seated room
  2. Separate Hostel for 1st year student
  3. Breakfast, Lunch, Tiffin and dinner are available
  4. High Speed Wi-Fi Campus
  5. 24x7 CCTV surveillance
  6. 24x7 Electricity & Water facility
  7. Purified drinking water
  8. Geyser in bathroom
  9. Emergency medical support
  10. Emergency vehicle for medical purpose
  11. Separate Library in Hostel
  12. Well-equipped Gymnasium
  13. Indoor Outdoor Games
  14. Social activities like tree plantation, blood donation camp etc.
  15. Regular Cleaning facility
  16. Anti-Ragging Squad
  17. All Time Hostel Superintendent present
  18. Round the clock Security service

Hostel Wardens who are teachers, are appointed for looking after the boarders of each hostel. Every day Wardens visit the hostel. For any kind of help Boarders as well as their parents should consult their necessity with the warden.
