Welcome to College Hostel

The aim of our Hostel is to make it a Home away from Home with focused education. The air-cooled hostel has a pleasant ambiance with the main focus on studies and bringing out latent qualities of the students by promoting sports, cultural activities and interpersonal development. Our hostel provides a comfortable and affordable accommodation for college students. We offer a range of facilities including a gym, library, and common room.

About Our Hostel

Asansol Engineering College Hostels is a popular accommodation option for engineering students studying in Asansol. The hostel is known for its modern amenities, comfortable living spaces, and affordable pricing making choice for students seeking a home away from home. The hostel provides a safe and secure environment for students, with round-the-clock security personnel stationed at the premises. It offers facilities such as a common room,study area,cafe,mess hall and recreational facilities to ensure that students can enjoy a well-rounded experience.
